Privacy Policy

Catholic Daily Scripture

Apple App Store Google Play Store

Effective & Last Updated: May 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Catholic Daily Scripture ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our application, available on the Apple App Store and the Google Play Store. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

1. Information the App Uses

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not require account creation, login, or any personal details to use the App.

The App displays banner advertising through Google AdMob, configured for non-personalized ads. The App does not request the AppTrackingTransparency (ATT) permission or access the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) on iOS. Google may still process limited technical data automatically, including:

  • Device information and identifiers
  • Approximate location (based on IP address)
  • Device type, operating system, and language
  • Ad interaction and diagnostics data

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us directly.

If you choose to share a scripture passage, the App sends that passage text to your device's native share tools only when you initiate that action. No data is transmitted to our servers.

· · ✦ · ·

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • The App does not offer or require user accounts, login, or sign-up.
  • The App does not store personal devotional journal entries on our servers.
  • The App does not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any identifying information.
  • The App does not use analytics SDKs beyond what AdMob provides as part of its ad serving.

3. How Information Is Used

Any data collected by AdMob is used by Google to:

  • Serve advertisements within the App
  • Measure ad performance and effectiveness
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties for purposes beyond advertising delivery through AdMob.

· · ✦ · ·

4. Third-Party Services

This App integrates Google AdMob to serve non-personalized banner ads. Google may collect and process data under its own policies.

  • Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Ad Technology Practices: policies.google.com/technologies/ads
iOS (App Store) note: AdMob's data practices on iOS are also disclosed via the App's Privacy Nutrition Label on the App Store product page, as required by Apple. That label reflects the data AdMob may collect as a third-party SDK integrated into this App.

We encourage you to review Google's privacy policy to understand how your data is handled.

5. Data Security

Any data transmitted by or on behalf of this App — such as ad request data sent to Google AdMob — is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. Catholic Daily Scripture itself does not operate servers that store your personal data. We rely on the security practices of third-party providers (Google) for data they collect.

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of any information processed by third-party services.

6. Children's Privacy

Catholic Daily Scripture is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or under 16 where required by local law, such as GDPR). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this App, please contact us at the address below and we will take appropriate steps to remove that information.

7. Data Retention

Catholic Daily Scripture does not maintain user accounts or store personal data on its own servers. Retention of any data collected by third-party ad and analytics providers is governed by their respective policies.

8. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your region, you may have certain rights regarding personal data. Here is how to exercise your choices:

  • You may stop using the App at any time by uninstalling it from your device.
  • You may choose not to use the share feature.
  • You may manage ad and privacy preferences through your device settings, your Google account settings, and the in-app Ad Privacy Choices button when Google requires that option.
  • iOS: This App does not request ATT permission because it is configured for non-personalized ads and does not access the IDFA.
  • Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.
  • Ad data deletion: To request deletion or opt-out of ad-related data Google may have associated with your device, visit Google My Ad Center.
  • If you are located in the EEA, United Kingdom, or California (CCPA), you may have additional rights regarding your personal data. Contact us at the address below to exercise any such rights.

9. Jurisdiction & Governing Law

Catholic Daily Scripture is operated from the United States. By using this App, you acknowledge that any information processed in connection with the App is subject to United States law. If you are using the App from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be processed in the United States or other countries.
