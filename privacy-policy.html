Catholic Daily Scripture Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

Catholic Daily Scripture provides daily Catholic scripture passages and devotional reflections. The app is available on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. This policy explains what data the app may access, how it is used, and what third parties may receive it.

Information the app uses

  • The app does not require a user account, password, or profile to read scripture content.
  • The app shows banner advertising through Google AdMob. The app requests non-personalized ads and does not request AppTrackingTransparency permission or access the IDFA.
  • Google may still process limited data such as device information, diagnostics, approximate location, and ad interaction data according to Google’s policies.
  • If you choose to share a scripture passage, the app sends that passage text to your device’s native share tools only when you initiate the action.

Information we do not currently collect

  • The current version of the app does not provide user accounts.
  • The current version of the app does not require login or sign-up.
  • The current version of the app does not store personal devotional journal entries on our servers.

Third-party services

This app uses Google AdMob to serve non-personalized banner ads. Google may collect and process data under its own policies. Review Google’s privacy information here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data retention

Catholic Daily Scripture does not currently maintain user accounts or store personal profile data on an app server. Third-party ad and analytics retention is governed by the policies of those providers.

Your choices

  • You may stop using the app at any time.
  • You may choose not to use the share feature.
  • You may manage ad and privacy preferences through your device settings, Google account settings, and the in-app Ad Privacy Choices button when Google requires that option.
  • The iOS (App Store) version of the app does not request tracking permission because it is configured for non-personalized ads and does not access the IDFA.

Contact

For questions or privacy requests about Catholic Daily Scripture, contact BDTTalentGroup@yahoo.com. The Apple App Store and Google Play Store listings use this same support contact.
