Privacy Policy

Catholic Daily Scripture

Effective Date: April 17, 2026  |  Last Updated: April 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Catholic Daily Scripture ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our Android application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

1. Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not require account creation, login, or any personal details to use the App.

However, the App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain data automatically, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID)
  • Approximate location (based on IP address)
  • Device type, operating system, and language
  • App usage and interaction data for ad targeting

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us directly.

· · ✦ · ·

2. How Information Is Used

Any data collected by AdMob is used by Google to:

  • Serve relevant advertisements within the App
  • Measure ad performance and effectiveness
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties for purposes beyond advertising delivery through AdMob.

3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying banner advertisements. AdMob's data practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy, available at policies.google.com/privacy.

We encourage you to review Google's privacy policy to understand how your data is handled by their services.

· · ✦ · ·

4. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through this App, please contact us so we can take appropriate steps to remove such data.

5. Data Retention

Since we do not collect personal data ourselves, we retain no user data. Any data collected by AdMob is subject to Google's own retention policies.

6. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your region, you may have certain rights regarding personal data, including the right to access, correct, or request deletion of data held by third parties like Google. You may opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting the settings on your Android device:

  • Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

You may also reset your Advertising ID at any time from the same menu.
